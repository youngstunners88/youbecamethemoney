# Wednesday Demo Script — Mr. Garcia

**Duration:** 5 minutes  
**Goal:** Mr. Garcia says yes  
**URL:** [Vercel deployment link]  
**Browser:** Chrome (required for voice)

---

## Before You Start

- [ ] Open dashboard URL in Chrome
- [ ] Open interview portal in separate tab: `/portal`
- [ ] Turn on sound
- [ ] Close other apps — full screen the browser

---

## Minute 1 — The Hook (Live Leads)

> "Mr. Garcia, this is your command center. Everything Hermes does — every lead, every call, every case — lives here."

**Click:** Live Leads tab (default)

Point out:
- 15 leads streaming in, each with urgency score
- Click any lead → CaseDetail opens: interaction log, case notes, pipeline stage
- "Every time someone messages you on Telegram or WhatsApp, they appear here instantly."

**Click back** to Live Leads.

---

## Minute 2 — The Pipeline

> "You can see exactly where each prospect is in your sales process."

**Click:** Pipeline tab

Point out:
- Kanban columns: New → Qualified → Consulting → Retained → Closed
- Drag a card from New to Qualified — it moves live
- "Hermes moves leads automatically based on what they say."

---

## Minute 3 — The Interview Portal

> "Here's the new piece. Before Margarita even calls, leads go through a short interview. Watch."

**Switch to portal tab** (`localhost:8090/portal` or deployed URL)

Walk through 2–3 questions quickly:
- Situation → matter type → urgency
- "The system is scoring their confidence, urgency, knowledge level in real time."
- Submit → show result screen with warmth score

> "Margarita now calls knowing this is a hot lead with a $97K UCC matter in California."

---

## Minute 4 — The Command Center

**Click:** Admin button (top right) → opens Command Center

Show 3 tabs in sequence:

**🌡️ Warmth tab:**
> "These are leads ranked by their interview score. Hot leads get called first."

**🏆 Hall of Fame tab:**
> "Every case you win gets anonymized and featured here. Builds social proof automatically."

**📈 Growth tab:**
> "This is what Layer 3 does for your business. Right now your close rate is 18.5%. By week 8, Hermes has learned enough to push it to 35%. That's an extra $7,900 a week — for a system that costs less than a dollar a week to run."

---

## Minute 5 — The Close

**Click:** Hermes tab (inside Command Center)

Point to 3-month projections:
- Lead intake: +200%
- Acquisition cost: −35%
- Revenue potential: $120K+/month

> "This is week 1. The system learns. Every interview, every call, every case outcome makes Margarita smarter. You don't hire more staff — the AI gets better."

Pause.

> "The setup is $3,500. After that, $1,200 a month. At your current close rate, one retained client pays for 3 months."

---

## If He Asks Questions

**"Is the data real?"**
> "This is the prototype with demo data. Week 1 integration connects your real Telegram, WhatsApp, and Retell account. Every lead you have today will flow in."

**"Who owns this?"**
> "You do. It runs on your VPS. Your database. Your data. I don't touch it after handoff."

**"What if it breaks?"**
> "The $1,200/month includes monitoring and maintenance. Plus Hermes has rule-based fallbacks — if the AI is down, it keeps working."

**"Can I customize it?"**
> "Every skill — lead scoring, interview questions, case patterns — is built around your practice. UCC Article 3, securitization, trust verification. Not generic law. Your law."
